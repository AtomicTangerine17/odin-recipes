Spaghetti Bolognese - The Quinn way

Description

Gorgeous spaghetti bolognese just like your granny use to make for you.
With fresh ingredients this recipe is to die for.
This recipe can serve upto 6 people.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 4 rashers smoked streaky bacon, finely chopped
  • 2 medium onions, finely chopped
  • 2 carrots, trimmed and finely chopped
  • celery sticks, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves finely chopped
  • 2-3 sprigs rosemary leaves picked and finely chopped
  • 500g beef mince

For the bolognese sauce

  • 2x 400g tins plum tomatoes
  • small pack basil
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 2 tbsp tomato puree
  • 1 beef stock
  • 125ml red wine
  • 6 cherry tomatoes

To season and serve

  • 75g parmesan
  • 400g Spaghetti

Method to the madness

  1. Put a large saucepan on a medium heat and add 1tbsp olive oil.
  2. Add 4 finely chopped bacon rashers and fry for 10mins.
  3. Reduce the heat and add the 2 onions, 2 carrots,2 celery sticks, 2 garlic cloves and the leaves from
    2-3 sprigs rosemary, all finely chopped, then fry for 10 mins. Stir the veg often until it softens.
  4. Increase the heat to medium-high, add the 500g mince and cook for 3-4mins until browned all over
  5. Add 2 tins plum tomatoes, the finely chopped leaves from ¾ small pack basil, 1 tsp dried oregano, 2 bay leaves,
    2 tbsp tomato purée, 1 beef stock cube, 125ml red wine and 6 halved cherry tomatoes. Stir with a wooden spoon, breaking up the plum tomatoes.
  6. Bring to boil, reduce to a gentle simmer and cover wiha lid. Cook for 1hr 15mins while stirring occasionally.
  7. Add the 75g parmesan.
  8. When the sauce is nearly done, cook the spaghetti following the pack instructions.
  9. Drain the spaghetti and either stir into sauce, or serve the sauce on top.
